Designed for experience design teams

Use Literate Lamp as the visual OS for pop-up exhibitions, themed cafés, or fandom nights. With one CSS file, you can orchestrate typography, lighting cues, and ambient textures across every page.

  • Micro-interactions: Motion-tuned transitions plus theme-specific accents.
  • Accessibility: Contrast-aware palettes and reduced motion support.
  • Performance: Zero framework, no heavy dependencies, deferred assets.
